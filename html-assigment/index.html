About Me

My name is Jeffrey Arbutante, a self-taught graphic designer based in the Philippines. I finished my degree in computer science, but I didn't use it in my professional experience, I have significant experience in the field of graphic design. I was hired as a Graphic Designer where I also spent my internship. Highlights of my career as a Graphic Designer are securing various design campaigns from one of the biggest retailers in the country, where my designs are featured in different collaterals like billboards, newspapers, and product catalogs.

During my free time, I enjoy activities such as cycling and skating with friends. I love to travel, explore new places, and meet new people. and I also have a passion for photography. I work as a part-time photographer even during weekends, capturing moments at events such as weddings, parties, and even music events.
